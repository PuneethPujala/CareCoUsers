## ❤️ 1. VITALS & WELL-BEING UPDATES

### A. Positive Reinforcement (Green Day Notifications)
- "Morning, [Name]! Your heart rate and sleep patterns were excellent last night. You’re off to a great start today! ☀️"
- "That’s the spirit! Your resting heart rate is [XX] bpm—well within the perfect range. Your heart is happy! ❤️"
- "Night owl or early bird? Either way, your sleep quality last night was superb. Keep it up, [Name]! 🌙✨"
- "All vitals looking green! Your blood oxygen levels are perfect—you’re breathing easy, and so are we. 🌿"
- "Fantastic news! Your stress levels are at their lowest this week. Whatever you’re doing, keep doing it! 💆‍♂️"
- "[Name], you’ve had the best BP reading in 3 weeks. This is real progress—your body is responding beautifully! 📈"
- "Wake-up call—the good kind! Your morning vitals show you’re energized and ready for the day ahead. Go shine! 🌟"
- "Weekend warrior energy detected! Your vitals show you’re in great form—ready for activity and adventure! 🏃"

### B. Gentle Nudges & Micro-Interventions
- "We noticed a small spike in your heart rate this morning—probably just the coffee! But let’s sip some water too. Hydration helps! 💧"
- "Your activity level dropped yesterday. No pressure, but even a 5-minute stroll later today would be perfect for you! 🚶"
- "Psst… we’re seeing your stress levels creep up slightly. How about taking 3 deep breaths right now? It actually helps! 🌬️"
- "Your sleep was shorter than usual last night. Feeling okay? Try to catch a nap or aim for 7 hours tonight. We’re watching out for you! 😴"
- "Temperature check: Your body temp is slightly elevated. Stay cool and hydrated today, okay? 🌡️"
- "Gentle reminder: Your vitals show dehydration signs. That water bottle isn’t going to drink itself! 💦"

### C. Trend Reports & Insight Stories
- "Weekly Wrap-up: You’ve been very consistent this week. Your average resting heart rate is looking strong! 📊"
- "30-Day Check-In: Your overall health score has improved by 12% compared to last month. You’re winning at wellness! 🏆"
- "Seasonal Shift: Your vitals are more stable now that the weather is cooler. Interesting pattern—we’re tracking this! 🍂"
- "Anniversary Milestone: A year ago today, your BP was 142/92. Today it’s [Current]. That’s incredible progress, [Name]! 🎉"

### D. Comparative & Social Vitals
- "How are you compared to others your age? Your heart rate variability puts you in the ‘Excellent’ range among the fittest! 💪"
- "[Name], your daily step count today beat your personal average by 15%. That’s a new personal record! 🥇"

## 💊 2. MEDICATION & DELIVERY LOGISTICS

### A. Pre-Medication Reminders
- "Good morning! Time for your vitamins. They’re already set out—do you see them? 💊"
- "[Caretaker Name] has a reminder: Your BP meds are due in 30 minutes. Let’s stay on schedule! ⏰"
- "Lunch is coming up! Don’t forget your [Medicine Name] with food—it’s time. 🍽️"
- "Your body’s asking for its evening dose. Take it now with a light snack for best results. 🌙"
- "Medication o’clock! Your [Medicine Name] is ready. We’ve set a gentle phone reminder too. 📱"
- "[Name], your [Medicine Name] is due in 15 minutes. Perfect time to grab a glass of water and settle down. 💧"

### B. Delivery Excitement & Logistics
- "Great news! Your monthly vitamins and prescriptions are out for delivery. Track live here! 🚚"
- "Your medicine order just left our warehouse! That’s [X] items coming your door. Expected delivery: 2–4 PM today. 📦"
- "Your delivery is 15 minutes away! [Driver Name] is almost at your door. 👀"
- "Package delivered! Your medicines are at your door. [Caretaker Name] will help unpack them. 📬"
- "Smooth sailing! Your refill order was fast-tracked. Arrives tomorrow by 10 AM—faster than expected! ⚡"
- "Special delivery alert: Your insulin pack has arrived with ice packs. Keep it cool! ❄️"

### C. Refill & Stock Management
- "Refill Reminder: You’re down to your last 5 doses of [Medicine Name]. We’re already queuing your next order! 🔁"
- "Stock Check: Only 3 days left of your meds. We’re ordering more—no gaps in coverage! 📉"
- "Your [Medicine Name] refill is 70% done. Next batch is being prepared. Auto-refill set for [Date]. 🔄"
- "URGENT-ish: Your medicine runs out in 2 days. Refill approved and dispatching tomorrow. You’re covered! 🚨"
- "Seasonal refill incoming! Allergy meds ready due to pollen season. Delivery by [Date]. 🌸"

### D. Medical Records & Documentation
- "New records added! Your medical history is updated with latest lab results. 📁"
- "Your prescription history was updated and shared with [Doctor Name]. Approved! ✔️"
- "Milestone: 60 consecutive days of medication compliance! That’s amazing! 🎯"

### E. Safety & Interaction Alerts
- "Reminder: Take [Medicine A] at least 2 hours apart from [Medicine B]. You’re doing great! ⚠️"
- "Good call! Taking meds with dairy improves absorption. Keep it up! 🥛"
- "Interaction check passed! Your supplement is safe with current meds. Verified by your doctor. ✔️"

## 👨‍⚕️ 3. HUMAN TOUCH & CARETAKER NOTIFICATIONS

### A. Pre-Call Warmups
- "[Name], [Caretaker] will call in 15 mins. Get comfy—they’re excited to chat! ☎️"
- "Heads up! Morning call in 10 minutes. Tea + chat time! ☕"
- "[Caretaker] checked in and will call in 20 mins. They have good news! 😊"
- "Quick check: Call starts in 25 mins. Anything you want to ask? 🤔"
- "Alert! Caretaker is online now. Press ‘Available Now’ to talk early! 🟢"

### B. Post-Call Reinforcement
- "Great chat today! Don’t forget your evening meds with a snack. 🍎"
- "You sounded great today! Don’t forget ankle exercises—3 sets daily. 💪"
- "Recap: Schedule a follow-up with your cardiologist. Added to calendar. 📅"
- "You reported [Symptom]. Your doctor has been notified. 🩺"
- "Voice note from caretaker added—listen anytime! 🎧"

### C. Caretaker Personality Touches
- "New caretaker alert! [Name] is excited to work with you. 🤝"
- "It’s your caretaker’s birthday! Wish them when they call 🎂"
- "They’re back from vacation and missed talking to you! 💙"
- "Fun fact: They love [hobby]! You both might click! 🎨"

### D. Missed Calls
- "Missed your call? No worries. Reschedule in 30 mins. ⏳"
- "Caretaker couldn’t reach you. Rescheduled for 4 PM. 👍"
- "Morning check-in in 5 mins. Feeling okay? 🤍"

### E. Special Occasions
- "Happy Birthday, [Name]! 🎉"
- "Congrats! Your caretaker shared great news about you! 🥳"
- "You’re stronger than you think. We’re rooting for you! 💚"

## 📊 11. NOTIFICATION STRATEGY GUIDELINES

### Frequency
- **Vitals**: Daily
- **Meds**: Before dose
- **Calls**: 15–30 min prior
- **Milestones**: 2–3/week
- **Family**: 2–3/week

### Personalization Tokens
- `[Name]`, `[Caretaker]`, `[Medicine]`, `[Metric]`, `[Time]`, `[Date]`

### Tone Rules
- Encouraging
- Supportive
- Simple
- Actionable
- Warm
- Timely

### Best Practices
- Always give context
- Use real data
- Personalize deeply
- Avoid spam

---

## ❌ 12. NOTIFICATIONS TO AVOID
- Generic alerts
- Fear-based messages
- Medical jargon
- Too many notifications
- No personalization
- No context
- Competitive pressure
- Late-night alerts
